# Wealth Orbit Features

This document outlines the core features implemented in the Wealth Orbit application.

## 📊 Dashboard & Projections
- **Comprehensive Net Worth Tracking**: View your real-time net worth based on current assets and liabilities.
- **Interactive Projections Chart**: Visualize your wealth growth over custom timeframes (e.g., 5, 10, 30 years).
- **Toggleable Chart Layers**: Independently view Assets, Liabilities, Net Worth, and FIRE targets on the projection curve.
- **Responsive Layout**: Mobile-first design ensures the dashboard and complex charts work flawlessly on any device.

## 💰 Asset Management
- **Manual Logging**: Add custom assets with detailed parameters.
- **Growth Projections**: Specify expected annual return rates for each asset.
- **Interest Types**: Support for both Simple and Compound interest calculations based on the asset type.
- **Asset Categorization**: Group assets by type (e.g., Equity, Real Estate, Liquid).

## 💳 Liability Management
- **Debt Tracking**: Log loans, mortgages, and other liabilities.
- **Amortization**: Automatically calculates principal paydown based on Interest Rate and EMI.
- **Debt-Free Projection**: Visually projects the exact year you will become debt-free.

## 🔥 FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early) Planning
- **Detailed Settings**: Configure Annual Expenses, Inflation Rate, Safe Withdrawal Rate, and Life Expectancy.
- **Moving Goalpost Visualization**: The projection chart includes an inflation-adjusted "FIRE Target" line, showing exactly when your growing wealth intersects with your inflating living costs.
- **Continuous Recalculation**: Decoupled from the explicit chart view to ensure your "Retire By" year is always accurate regardless of the projection window being viewed.

## 🔒 Security & User Experience
- **User Authentication**: Secure login and registration flows handling individual user profiles.
- **Privacy-First**: Your financial data is tied securely to your account.
- **Dark Mode Support**: Aesthetic, glassmorphism-inspired UI with seamless dark and light mode transitions.
